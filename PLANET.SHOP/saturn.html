Saturn

Saturn, the sixth planet from the Sun, is renowned for its stunning ring system, which is composed of ice and rock particles.
It is the second-largest planet in our solar system, classified as a gas giant, primarily made up of hydrogen and helium. Saturn has over 80 moons, with Titan being the largest, showcasing a dense atmosphere and liquid methane lakes. The planet's unique hexagonal storm at its north pole and its low density, which allows it to float in water, make it a fascinating object of study.
