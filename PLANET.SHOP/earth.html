Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known place in the universe that supports life. Its diverse environment includes vast oceans, landmasses, and an atmosphere rich in oxygen and nitrogen.
Earth has a balanced climate, with a protective ozone layer shielding it from harmful solar radiation. It experiences seasons due to its axial tilt, and its single moon affects ocean tides. Earth is home to millions of species.
