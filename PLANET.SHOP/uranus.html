Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is unique for its sideways rotation, making it the only planet that rotates on its side. A gas giant, it has a bluish hue due to methane in its atmosphere, which absorbs red light.
Uranus has 27 known moons and a faint ring system. Its extreme axial tilt leads to unusual seasons, with each pole experiencing 42 years of sunlight followed by 42 years of darkness.
